    Frequently Asked Questions

    What is the eligibility criteria for UGC NET JRF?

    The candidate should have a Master’s degree with at least 55% marks or equivalent grade from a recognized university.

    What is the exam pattern for UGC NET CSIR?

    The exam consists of a single paper with MCQs, divided into Part A, B, and C. The exam duration is 3 hours.

    How many attempts are allowed for UPSC Civil Services Examination?

    The number of attempts varies according to the category of the candidate. General category candidates can attempt the exam six times, while OBC candidates can attempt it nine times. There is no limit for SC/ST candidates.

    Is CTET necessary to become a teacher in India?

    Yes, CTET is mandatory for a candidate who wants to teach Classes I to VIII in a central government school in India.

    What is the selection process for CUET?

    The selection process for CUET includes a written test and an interview.

    What is the exam duration for UGC NET JRF?

    The exam duration for UGC NET JRF is 3 hours.

    Is there negative marking in UGC NET CSIR?

    Yes, there is negative marking in UGC NET CSIR. For each wrong answer, 0.25 marks are deducted.

    How many papers are there in the UPSC Civil Services Examination?

    There are two papers in the UPSC Civil Services Examination – Preliminary and Main.
